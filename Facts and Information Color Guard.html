Facts and Information

Color guard is a group that uses props such as flags, ribbons, and rifles along with expressive dance movements and music.



How to Join

  • Talk to Austin Paxman at Pine View High School if you are interested.

Competitions/Activities

  • We do various competitions, often going up north every other weekend and getting experience ranked by judges.

Commitment

  • You will have to be committed and willing to show up on time to 6 a.m. practice and some after school practices. It involves a lot of practicing and extra time.

Who can Join

  • Anyone who wants to! No experience required!

Winter Guard

  • Winter guard starts after the marching band season. it is focused only on the color guard team, who dances with props along to a music track.

Fees

  • Fees may range up to about $250 for costumes and other expenses.
